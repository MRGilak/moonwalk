#Control #LinearControlSystems 
In this note, I am going to explain polar diagrams. To plot the polar diagram of a system, we insert $j \omega$ instead of $s$ in its transfer function. Then, we separate the real and imaginary parts of the result and plot the result in the complex plane while varying $\omega$ from $0$ to $\infty$.
