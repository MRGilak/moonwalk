#Control #NonlinearControl
I have finally decided to write a series of notes on nonlinear control as well. Nonlinear control has always been my favorite subject and the course I had was one of the best courses I've had ever (taught by Professor Mehrzad Namvar at Sharif University of Technology, spring 2025).
The notes are mainly based on the material of the course I had plus a noticeable touch of intuition that I've added myself. I think this intuition actually helps grasp the mathematics and why we're doing what we're doing much better.
For now, I am skipping the classic part of nonlinear control in the notes (Lyapunov-based control design) and I am going straight to the subject of _geometric approach to nonlinear control_, which is based on _differential geometry_. If you are not familiar with differential geometry, you don't need to worry, because I have tried to cover even the basics; however, prior knowledge in this field will still benefit you.
